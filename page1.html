FORTUNA EDUHEALTH CENTER

Fortuna EduHealth Center is a learning institution accredited with an 'A' by the Ministry of Health of Indonesia established in 2024.

Fortuna focuses on enhancing the skills and competencies of Indonesian healthcare professionals. We provide training, seminars, and workshops tailored to healthcare workers to support their professional development.

Vision

To become the leading learning institution in Indonesia, committed to empowering healthcare professionals with advanced skills and knowledge, contributing to the overall improvement of healthcare services in the nation.

Mission

1.  To provide high-quality, accredited training programs that enhance the competencies of healthcare professionals.

2.  To organize seminars and workshops that promote continuous professional development and address the evolving needs of the healthcare sector.

3.  To foster an environment of lifelong learning, supporting healthcare workers in delivering excellent, patient-centered care.

4.  To collaborate with experts and institutions to ensure the latest best practices and innovations in healthcare are accessible to all professionals.
